Specialised Neuro & Respiratory

Moving beyond general MSK to high-acuity needs: MND progression, Spasticity management, and community-based Respiratory Physio.

Analytical Documentation

Scheme-ready reporting. We link impairments to functional goals and justify non-delegable therapy tasks for NDIS & LTCS funding.

Person-Centred Practice

A holistic approach integrating physical, psychological, and environmental factors. Essential for the effective management of chronic pain and progressive neurological conditions.

Long-Term Partnership

Committed to continuity of care. We establish stable, enduring therapeutic relationships to support evolving clinical needs across the Greater Western Sydney region.

Clinical Specialisations

Expertise focused on functional independence and complex care management.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Targeted management for specific complex conditions.

  • MND progression monitoring
  • Stroke (Spasticity/relearning)
  • TBI / ABI & SCI Rehabilitation
  • Gait & Balance training

Respiratory Physiotherapy

High-acuity care often required in neuro/MND cases.

  • MND respiratory decline patterns
  • Cough assist & Suction techniques
  • NIV coordination
  • Chest clearance management

Complex Disability (LTCS)

Long-term management of catastrophic injury.

  • Standing frame systems
  • 24-hour positioning & pressure care
  • Equipment prescription
  • Carer training & education

Chronic Pain Management

Holistic, Evidence-Based Chronic Pain Management.

  • Biopsychosocial assessment
  • Graded motor imagery
  • Pacing & fatigue management
  • Self-efficacy building
